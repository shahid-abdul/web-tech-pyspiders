About

When you shop our BMW M Series inventory, you may be wondering what the BMW M Series is and what it means. The “M” in the BMW M Series stands for “Motorsport”, and the M Series was originally created to facilitate BMW's racing program. Over time, the BMW M program began to supplement their vehicle lineup with modified vehicle models, which are now available to the general public. These new M Series vehicles have modified engines, transmissions, suspensions, interior trims, aerodynamics, and exterior modifications to set them apart from their BWM counterparts. So, when you get behind the wheel of a BMW M Series vehicle, you're driving a top-of-the-line performance model that was manufactured to the highest standards of the BMW lineup.

BMW Motorrad is the motorcycle brand and division of German automotive manufacturer, BMW.It has produced motorcycles since 1923, and achieved record sales for the fifth year in succession in 2015. With a total of 136,963 vehicles sold in 2015, BMW registered a growth of 10.9% in sales in comparison with 2014.In May 2011, the 2,000,000th motorcycle produced by BMW Motorrad was an R1200GS.

This combination of technical perfection with highly emotional sports performance will raise your heart rate. BMW M models are real sports cars. From saloons and coupés to SUVs, racing technology such as M sports suspension, engines, and typical M tuning make these sporty vehicles the ultimate blend of luxury and performance.
